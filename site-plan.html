Naira2CFA Website Site Plan

Planning document for a cross-border currency exchange platform

Site Name

Name: Naira2CFA

Rationale: This name directly reflects the platform’s purpose—facilitating currency exchange between Nigerian Naira and West African CFA Franc.

Site Purpose

  • Real-time exchange rates between NGN and XOF
  • Secure and fast currency conversion services
  • Financial literacy resources and compliance guidance
  • Support for account creation, verification, and beneficiary management

Scenarios

  • What is the current exchange rate from Naira to CFA Franc?
  • How fast will my funds arrive in Benin after initiating a transfer?
  • How do I verify my account for compliance?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Green (#006A2B)
  • Accent Gold (#CFAE49)
  • Background Beige (#F9F8F6)
  • Text Black (#1A1A1A)

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and titles to create a modern, bold look
  • Roboto: Used for body text and paragraphs for readability and clarity
